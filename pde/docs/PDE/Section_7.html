    def Chapter7.solvesHeatEq (α : ℝ) (u : ℝ × ℝ → ℝ) :
    Prop

    In one space dimension the diffusion equation is given for u(x, t) by

    A function u : ℝ × ℝ → ℝ satisfies the 1D heat (diffusion) equation with constant α : ℝ if for all (t,x), the time derivative of u equals α times the second spatial derivative of u.
