    Maximum principle for convolution with a nonnegative unit-mass kernel

    Assume:

    • t > 0;
    • the product and the kernel are integrable: Integrable (fun y ↦ Φ (x - y) t * g y) and Integrable (fun y ↦ Φ (x - y) t);
    • the initial datum is bounded by B: |g y| ≤ B for all y.

    Then |u(x,t)| ≤ B, where u = heatConvolution Φ g.

    This is the precise Lean 4 formalization of the book’s calculation |u| ≤ ∫ Φ |g| ≤ B ∫ Φ = B.
