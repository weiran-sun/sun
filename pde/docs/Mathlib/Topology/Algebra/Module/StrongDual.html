    The topological strong dual of a module #

    Main definitions #

    • strongDualPairing: The strong dual pairing as a bilinear form.
    • StrongDual.polar: Given a subset s in a monoid M (over a commutative ring R), the polar polar R s is the subset of StrongDual R M consisting of those functionals which evaluate to something of norm at most one at all points z ‚àà s.
    • StrongDual.polarSubmodule: Given a subset s in a monoid M (over a field ùïú) closed under scalar multiplication, the polar polarSubmodule ùïú s is the submodule of StrongDual ùïú M consisting of those functionals which evaluate to zero at all points z ‚àà s.
